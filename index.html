Business Law

Business lawyers often develop expertise in one or more specialized areas of practice, such as those described below.

Criminal Law

As a lawyer who practices criminal law, you will either prosecute criminal cases or defend those accused of crimes.

Child Support

Child support is the amount of money that a court tells a parent to pay every month.

Education Law

Legal education is the education of individuals in the principles, practices, and theory of law.

Divorce Law

In a divorce the couples or the judge may need to make decisions on five areas; child custody, child support, division of assets, division of debts, and spousal support.

Tax Law

Tax lawyers are involved in advising their clients (such as private individuals, companies, or the government) on the best way to mitigate their tax liability.
